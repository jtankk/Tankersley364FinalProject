On the next screen you will find an email inbox.

Please determine which emails are spam and which are safe to be kept.

You can open an email by clicking on the subject. Select whether you will keep or report an email as spam at the top. Once you've made your selection, you will be returned to your inbox. You can return to the inbox before making your selection by clicking the "back" button at the top of every screen.

Correctly idetifying an email as malicious or safe will earn you a point. Once you've voted to keep or report each email, you will be able to view your score.
